I am a first-year PhD student in Computer Science at University College London, where I am interested in developing Generalist AI agents that enhance their capabilities through synthetic data and demonstrate creativity in open-ended environments. I am supervised by Ilija Bogunovic and Jack Parker-Holder, and I am a member of UCL DARK Lab. Before starting my PhD at UCL, I completed a year-long deep learning research internship at HUAWEI R&D in Cambridge, UK, and hold an MSc in Artificial Intelligence from Leeds University. I have a background in motorsport engineering, but my passion is in creating machines that can think rather than race.
